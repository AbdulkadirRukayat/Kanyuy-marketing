About Muslim Bungalow

Expanded Concept

Muslim Bungalow is a serviced apartment that blends thoughtful design with everyday convenience. At its heart sits a central parlour — a shared, welcoming space where residents gather, connect, and unwind. From this parlour, three self-contained apartments branch out in a 3-in-1 layout, each with private entrance, kitchenette, bedroom, and bath, yet all tied seamlessly to the common hub.

Set within a vibrant Christian community, the design respects cultural sensitivities: prayer-friendly layouts, halal-ready kitchen zones, and modest finishes throughout. The central parlour remains open and neutral — a place for tea, conversation, or simply passing through.

Site Integration

The bungalow mirrors local aesthetics — pitched roofs, muted earth tones — so it feels like a natural extension rather than an intrusion. Community events can spill into the parlour courtyard when invited, turning the space into a bridge rather than a barrier.

Target Residents

  • Muslim professionals on mid-length assignments
  • Visiting families needing halal-friendly, serviced stays
  • Students or medical tourists seeking calm, central lodging
  • Anyone who values privacy plus the gentle hum of community
